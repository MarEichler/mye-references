12.1 Horizontal Spacing

Definition LaTeX mu* Result
Default 0 \(f q\)
Thin , or 3 \(f \, q\)
Medium 1 : 4 \(f \: q\)
Medium 2 ; 5 \(f \; q\)
Half of current font size, 0.5em 6 \(f \enspace q\)
Current font size, 1em 18 \(f \quad q\)
2x Current font size, 2em 36 \(f \qquad q\)
Space in normal text   NA \(f \ q\)
Negative space ! -3 \(f \! q\)
* 1em (current font size) = 18mu
